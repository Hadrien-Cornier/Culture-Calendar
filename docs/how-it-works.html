How It Works

Culture Calendar automatically collects and curates cultural events from Austin's premier venues, providing AI-powered ratings and personalized recommendations.

The Process

Every week, our system scrapes event listings from eight major Austin cultural venues. Each event is analyzed using advanced AI technology to provide ratings, summaries, and critical assessments. Events scheduled during standard work hours (9am-6pm weekdays) are automatically filtered out to focus on accessible cultural experiences.

Data Sources

We aggregate events from the following venues:

Film Venues

  • Austin Film Society — Art house and repertory cinema
  • Hyperreal Film Club — Experimental and avant-garde screenings
  • Paramount Theatre — Classic films and special events

Music Venues

  • Austin Symphony Orchestra — Classical symphonic performances
  • Texas Early Music Project — Medieval, Renaissance, and Baroque music
  • La Follia Austin — Chamber music ensemble

Literary Venues

  • Alienated Majesty Books — Book club and author events
  • First Light Austin — Multiple book clubs and literary discussions

AI Analysis

Each event is evaluated using Claude, an advanced AI system that considers artistic merit, historical significance, and cultural relevance. The system provides ratings on a 10-point scale along with detailed critical assessments written in an engaging, knowledgeable style.

Updates

The calendar updates automatically:

  • Weekly: Every Saturday at 9 PM UTC for incremental updates
  • Monthly: First of each month at 6 AM UTC for comprehensive refresh

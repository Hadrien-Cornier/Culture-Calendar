Wildsam x Texas State Parks Happy Hour
RSVP

Gather with us for a happy hour at First Light Books to celebrate the second release of Wildsam's Texas field guide with Texas State Parks.

Tickets include a copy of the updated field guide as well as a drink ticket. Free RSVPs are also encouraged.

About WILDSAM TEXAS

A field guide to Texas: Marfa art and Hill Country springs, songwriters and pitmasters, Texas traditions and Rio Grande stories.

Inside WILDSAM TEXAS, you'll find Lone Star tales from prize-winning writers, past and present; detailed road trip itineraries and profiles of Texas towns; interviews with painters, directors, activists and songwriters; delightful miscellany from the archives, selective recommendations about authentic Texas experiences and offbeat intel that offers stunning insight into Texas.

About Wildsam

Founded in 2012, Wildsam is an American travel brand built upon telling true stories of place. They seek out the real and rooted things, what’s truly authentic, and share the pathways to deep connections and wonders in the world, for travelers and locals alike.

About Texas State Parks

Since 1923, Texas State Parks has been dedicated to protecting the best parts of Texas’ vast natural and cultural beauty. More than 100 years later, the Texas State Park system has grown to a network of over 85 parks, historic sites and natural areas providing recreational educational opportunities to millions of visitors every year.

About Texas Parks & Wildlife Magazine

Since 1942, Texas Parks & Wildlife Magazine has been the voice of the Texas outdoors. Each issue of this award-winning publication is packed with outstanding photography and inspirational storytelling, shining the spotlight on a multitude of outdoor recreation opportunities including state parks, fishing, hunting, camping, birding, paddling, boating, and travel, as well as wildlife, conservation, and environmental issues across the Lone Star State.
